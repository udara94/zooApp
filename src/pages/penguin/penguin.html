penguin

Penguins are a group of aquatic, flightless birds. They live almost exclusively in the Southern Hemisphere, with only one species, the Galapagos penguin, found north of the equator.


  • Eats: Acacia

  • Higher classification: Sphenisciformes

  • Lifespan:
    • Emperor penguin: 20 years
    • Little penguin: 6 years

  • Mass:
    • Emperor penguin: 23 kg
    • Little penguin: 1.5 kg
    • Galapagos penguin: 2.5 kg

  • Height:
    • Emperor penguin: 1.1 – 1.3 m
    • Little penguin: 30 – 33 cm
    • Galapagos penguin: 49 cm

  • Scientific name: Spheniscidae
