lion

Lions live in social communities called prides.Female lions are responsible for hunting. A male’s loud roar can be heard as far as 5 miles away. Lions are excellent swimmers.Lions can consume as much as 60 pounds of food in one sitting. Lions spend about 20 hours a day resting, the rest of their time is spent hunting and eating.


  • Family: Felidae

  • Class: Mammalia

  • Kingdom: Animalia

  • Lifespan: 10 – 14 years

  • Scientific name: Panthera leo
