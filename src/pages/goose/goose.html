goose

Geese are waterfowl of the family Anatidae. This group comprises the genera Anser and Branta. Chen, a genus comprising 'white geese', is sometimes used to refer to a group of species that are more commonly placed within Anser.


  • Lifespan: Canada goose: 10 – 24 years

  • Higher classification: Anserinae

  • Mass: Canada goose: 3.2 – 6.5 kg

  • Breeds: Tula Fighting Goose

  • Clutch size:
    • Canada goose: 2 – 9
    • Swan goose: 5 – 6

  • Scientific name: Anserini
