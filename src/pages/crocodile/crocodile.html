crocodile

Crocodiles or true crocodiles are large aquatic reptiles that live throughout the tropics in Africa, Asia, the Americas and Australia. Crocodylinae, all of whose members are considered true crocodiles, is classified as a biological subfamily.


  • Kingdom: Animalia

  • Class: Reptilia

  • Scientific name: Crocodylinae

  • Lifespan:
    • Saltwater crocodile: 70 years
    • Nile crocodile: 70 – 100 years

  • Speed:
    • Saltwater crocodile: 24 – 29 km/h
    • Nile crocodile: 30 – 35 km/h
    • American crocodile: 32 km/h

  • Mass:
    • Saltwater crocodile: 400 – 1,000 kg
    • Nile crocodile: 220 – 550 kg
    • American crocodile: 400 – 500 kg

  • Length:
    • Saltwater crocodile: 4.3 – 5.2 m
    • Nile crocodile: 4.2 m
    • American crocodile: 4.1 – 4.8 m
